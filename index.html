About Mark Zuckerberg

关于马克·扎克伯格

Mark Elliot Zuckerberg, born on May 14, 1984, in White Plains, New York, is an American entrepreneur renowned for co-founding Facebook and its parent company, Meta Platforms. As of May 2025, his estimated net worth was $221.2 billion, positioning him as the world's second-richest individual.

马克·艾略特·扎克伯格（Mark Elliot Zuckerberg）于1984年5月14日出生于美国纽约州白原市，是社交媒体平台Facebook（现称Meta）的创始人、董事长兼首席执行官。他在哈佛大学就读期间，于2004年与室友共同创立了Facebook，旨在为大学生提供一个在线交流的平台。该平台迅速扩展，成为全球最受欢迎的社交媒体之一。

Timeline of Achievements

成就时间线

Founding of Facebook

创立Facebook

2004

While attending Harvard University, Zuckerberg launched Facebook alongside roommates Eduardo Saverin, Andrew McCollum, Dustin Moskovitz, and Chris Hughes.

在哈佛大学就读期间，扎克伯格与室友Eduardo Saverin、Andrew McCollum、Dustin Moskovitz和Chris Hughes共同创立了Facebook。

Donation to Newark Public Schools

向纽瓦克公立学校捐款

2010

Zuckerberg donated $100 million to Newark Public Schools, demonstrating his commitment to education.

扎克伯格向纽瓦克公立学校捐赠了1亿美元，展示了他对教育的承诺。

Acquisition of Instagram

收购Instagram

2012

Zuckerberg oversaw Facebook's acquisition of Instagram for $1 billion, which later grew to 3 billion monthly active users by 2025.

扎克伯格主导了Facebook以10亿美元收购Instagram的交易，到2025年，Instagram月活跃用户已增长至30亿。

Acquisition of Oculus VR

收购Oculus VR

2014

Recognizing the potential of virtual reality, Zuckerberg led Meta's acquisition of Oculus VR, advancing VR technology.

认识到虚拟现实的潜力，扎克伯格领导Meta收购了Oculus VR，推动了VR技术的发展。

Chan Zuckerberg Initiative

陈-扎克伯格倡议

2015

Zuckerberg and his wife, Priscilla Chan, established the Chan Zuckerberg Initiative (CZI), pledging to donate 99% of their Facebook shares over their lifetimes.

扎克伯格与妻子普莉希拉·陈共同创立了陈-扎克伯格倡议（CZI），承诺在他们的一生中捐出99%的Facebook股份。

Chan Zuckerberg Biohub

陈-扎克伯格生物中心

2016

Launched the Chan Zuckerberg Biohub with a $600 million endowment to foster collaboration between scientists at UC Berkeley, UCSF, and Stanford.

创立了陈-扎克伯格生物中心，投入6亿美元促进加州大学伯克利分校、加州大学旧金山分校和斯坦福大学科学家之间的合作。

Key Achievements

主要成就

Global Social Media Impact

全球社交媒体影响力

Under Zuckerberg's leadership, Facebook became a global social media giant, connecting billions worldwide.

在扎克伯格的领导下，Facebook成为了全球社交媒体巨头，连接了全球数十亿人。

Advancements in AI

人工智能进展

Zuckerberg has been instrumental in advancing artificial intelligence technologies within Meta, enhancing user experiences.

扎克伯格在推动Meta内部人工智能技术发展方面发挥了重要作用，提升了用户体验。

Philanthropic Impact

慈善影响

Through the Chan Zuckerberg Initiative, he has made significant contributions to education, healthcare, and scientific research.

通过陈-扎克伯格倡议，他在教育、医疗和科学研究领域做出了重大贡献。

Internet Accessibility

互联网可及性

Through Internet.org, Zuckerberg aimed to provide affordable internet access to underserved regions worldwide.

通过Internet.org计划，扎克伯格致力于为全球服务不足的地区提供负担得起的互联网接入。
